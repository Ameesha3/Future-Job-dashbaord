# Future of Jobs AI Dashboard
Project Description
This project is a **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze the **future of jobs, skill demand, and AI risk categories**.  
It provides insights into **job growth trends, salary benchmarks, skill demand scores, and AI-related risks** across countries, job titles, and education levels.  
The goal is to help organizations, policymakers, and professionals understand how **AI adoption impacts workforce evolution**.

Tech Stack
- **Power BI Desktop** – Data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive visuals  
- **CSV Dataset (Future of Jobs AI Dataset.csv)** – Job openings, skills, salaries, AI risk scores  
- **MS Excel** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **Visualization Techniques** – KPI cards, bar charts, line charts, pie charts, stacked charts, and slicers  

 Data Source
The dataset includes:
- **Job Titles** – Data Analyst, Business Analyst, Cloud Engineer, AI Researcher, Cybersecurity Analyst, ML Engineer, DevOps Engineer, etc.  
- **Skills** – Python, SQL, AWS, Docker, Deep Learning, Java, Excel, Security, Strategy  
- **Job Openings** – Historical (2015–2025) and projected (2030–2035)  
- **Education Levels** – Bachelor, Master, PhD  
- **Countries** – USA, UK, Germany, Canada, India, Australia  
- **AI Risk Categories** – High-risk vs Low-risk jobs  
- **Salary & Skill Demand Scores**  

 Features & Highlights

 Business Problem
Organizations and professionals need to understand **how AI adoption will reshape jobs** — which roles are at risk, which skills are in demand, and how salaries and growth trends will evolve.

 Goal of Dashboard
- Track **job growth % and AI risk scores**  
- Identify **top skills in demand** across industries  
- Compare **job openings by country and education level**  
- Visualize **future job openings by year and job title**  
- Provide **salary benchmarks and skill demand scores**  

 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
- **KPI Cards** – Avg AI Risk Score (40%), Job Growth % (-2.01%), Avg Salary (35K), Avg Skill Demand Score (79)  
- **Bar Chart** – Job openings by Job Title (Data Analyst, Cloud Engineer, ML Engineer, etc.)  
- **Skill Demand Grid** – Primary skills (Python, SQL, AWS, Docker, Deep Learning, etc.)  
- **Line Chart** – Job openings trend (2015–2035) by job title  
- **Pie Chart** – Job openings by Education Level (Bachelor, Master, PhD)  
- **Stacked Bar Chart** – Job openings by Country & AI Risk Category  
- **Bar Chart** – Job Level by Experience (Entry, Mid, Senior)  
- **Job Survival Analysis** – Job openings by survival class (0, 1, 2)  

 Business Impact & Insights
- **AI Risk:** 40% average risk score shows significant disruption potential.  
- **Job Growth:** Overall job growth is negative (-2.01%), highlighting automation risks.  
- **Skill Demand:** Python, SQL, AWS, and Deep Learning are top future skills.  
- **Education:** Majority of future jobs require Bachelor/Master degrees; PhD roles are niche but high-value.  
- **Country Insights:** USA and India show highest job openings, but also higher AI risk exposure.  
- **Job Trends:** Data Analysts and Cloud Engineers remain in demand, while some repetitive roles face decline.  

 Business Value
This dashboard enables:
- **Workforce planning** for organizations  
- **Skill development strategies** for professionals  
- **Policy insights** for governments on AI adoption impact  
- **Career guidance** for students and job seekers  

 Future Enhancements
- Add **forecasting models** for job demand prediction  
- Integrate **real-time labor market data** via APIs  
- Build **AI risk simulation models** for different industries
